# Executive Report: E-Commerce Data Insights
**Prepared by:** Hashem Al-Qurashi

## 1. Monthly Revenue Growth
Based on the SQL analysis, here are the key findings for the past year:
* **Highest Growth:** November 2025 saw a peak growth of **52.02%**.
* **Significant Drop:** January 2026 experienced a sharp decline of **-58.13%**, which is common after the holiday season.
* **Recovery:** Sales bounced back in February and March with **30.72%** and **61.26%** growth respectively.

## 2. Product Category Performance
The product categories were ranked by total revenue as follows:
1. **Electronics:** Lead the market with **$5,662,434.93**.
2. **Sports:** Second place with **$1,447,044.25**.
3. **Home & Kitchen:** **$1,388,319.02**.
*Note: Books are the lowest performing category with only $126,698.06.*

## 3. Recommendations
* **Focus on Electronics:** Since it's the main revenue driver, consider increasing the marketing budget for this category.
* **New Year Campaigns:** To mitigate the January slump, we should introduce early January sales next year.

## 4. Customer Retention & Detailed Trends
* **Loyalty:** Our April 2025 cohort remains the strongest, with **1,714** customers still active after 90 days.
* **Daily Stability:** Despite minor fluctuations, the 7-day moving average remains stable above **$50,000**, indicating healthy daily operations.
* **Milestone:** As of March 2026, the business has achieved a lifetime revenue of **$10,105,645.48**.

## 5. Final Recommendations
1. **Double Down on November:** Plan the next holiday campaign early, as November is historically our strongest growth month (52.02%).
2. **Retention Strategy:** Launch a re-engagement campaign for the January cohort to improve their lower-than-average retention rates.
3. **Inventory Management:** Align stock levels with the 7-day moving average to avoid overstocking during minor daily dips.
